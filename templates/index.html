Who We Are

Gruzo Maternity Clinic (GMC) started its operations on August 2009 at Sampaguita Street, Colongulo, Surallah, South Cotabato. It is owned by Faith G. Ugbana, a registered midwife practicing the profession for eighteen years. It started with one bed capacity and it expanded to three beds in 2016. Ms. Ugbana advocates that birthing homes must be brought nearer to the less fortunate, hence its location. She believes that this would help lessen the cases of infant and maternal mortality during giving birth. According to Ms. Ugbana, there is no better place to do this than to her own domicile. Barangay Colongulo is one of the 17 barangays in Surallah where the T’boli and the B’laan people live.
